What makes it powerful

Natural Language Control

Ask anything about your data in plain English or French. The AI understands context, formulas, and relationships across your sheets.

53 Smartsheet Tools

Read rows, add columns, update cells, manage permissions, sort, filter, cross-sheet references — every Smartsheet action at your fingertips.

7 AI Providers

Switch between OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, Groq, Mistral, DeepSeek, and OpenRouter. Choose your model in real-time.

Real-Time Streaming

Responses appear token by token. No waiting. Cancel mid-stream with the stop button. Conversations flow naturally.

Voice Dictation

Speak to your Smartsheet. Built-in Web Speech API support lets you dictate commands hands-free in any language.

Conversation History

Every conversation is saved locally. Switch between past sessions, export to Markdown, and pick up right where you left off.
